Recommended Study Method To memorize the questions and answers for the test, we recommend the following
  1. Read the question
  2. Read ONLY the correct answer. Highlight it somehow. Do not read the other answers - they are distractions.
  3. Repeat for all questions as long as it takes to recognize most of the correct answers.
Our brains are very good at recognition. So what the above process does is help you to recognize the correct answer.
